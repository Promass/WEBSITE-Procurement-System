User Guide

NVKE procurment system is a program that allows establishments and product suppliers to connect with each other and facilitate purchases, this guide will show you how to use the system

First, login using your credentials, you will be provided by both either by your superior who purchased the program or you'll initiall have the admin account with your first purchase

The type of your account is indicated in the top right corner as shown here

As a client, you can look at the list of items, and make requests to purchase them, if you're purchase is lower than $5000, then the system will automatically make the transaction for you, if not, then you will need your superior's approval before proceeding

If your designated account is of type "supplier", then once a purchase request's made, you'll see a new bubble pop in the highlighted area. You can submit your price and send it to the admin

As the admin, you have a bit more options, indicated by the red arrow to the left

In the home tab, you can look at the requested items that need approval where you can either accept or reject them

You can also see a list of all accounts, delete and create new ones as well

Aside from that, you can modify the product's list in the items tab

Finally, the history tab keeps track of all item requests made so far
