User Guide

NVKE procurment system is a program that allows establishments and product suppliers to connect with each other and facilitate purchases, this guide will show you how to use the system.

For testing puposes, we have these premade accounts that you can use to demo our system. Please avoid deleting these accounts.

First, login using the provided credentials.

The type of your account is indicated in the top right corner as shown here.

As a demander (customer), you can look at the list of items, and make requests to purchase them. Your pending requests will be in the 'Pending Demands' area while your completed requests will be in the 'Previous Request' area.

If your designated account is of type "supplier", then once a purchase request is made, you'll see a new bubble pop up in the highlighted area. You can submit an offer (quotation) which will be the price you want to sell this item for as a supplier. If the price is less than 5000$, then the quotation will be accepted by the system automatically. If the price is more than 5000$, thne the quotation will be sent to the admin for approval. The quotations waiting for admins approval will be in the 'Quotations Awaiting Approval' area. The completed quotations will be in the 'Previous Quotations' area.

As the admin, you have a bit more options, indicated by the red arrow to the left.

In the home tab, you can look at the requested items that need approval where you can either accept or reject them. You need to click on the demand blobs in the 'Pending Demands' area to see their information at the right. In the 'Revenue Data' area, you can see the total amount of money spent to purchase items from the supplier.

In the accounts tab, You can see a list of active accounts. You can delete accounts and create new ones. Note that deleting accounts will make them innactive only. They are still kept in the database for infomation purposes. You won't be able to create antoher account with the same username. This is an intentional feature.

In the items tab, you can see the list of active items. You can delete items and create new ones. Just like the accounts, deleting items will make them innactive only. They are still kept in the database for infomation purposes. You will be able to create antoher item with the same name. This is an intentional feature.

Finally, the history tab keeps track of all demands and quotations made so far.
